\section{Conclusion}

In the two day FoPra, we worked on various aspects of the TFI model and the phase transition property under different transverse field strengths $g$. Moreover, we analyzed the TFI model's correlations, entanglement structures and spectral properties.
\\\\
We conclude that the computational benefits of the tensor network approach for calculating these properties, such as the lower memory requirement and the speed-up from exponential to polynomial time do not negatively affect the precision of the obtained results, as the results we have obtained match the expectations. This means that the tensor network approach to solving similar problems is viable.
